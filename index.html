Stray Dog Awareness

Understanding and helping our street friends

"Adopt, Don't Shop"

Every stray deserves a loving home. When you adopt, you save a life and gain a loyal friend.

The Reality of Street Dogs in Nepal

Nepal is home to an estimated 3 million street dogs. These animals face numerous challenges including lack of food, shelter, and proper healthcare. Understanding their situation is the first step toward making a difference.

❓

Why Stray Dogs Exist

  • Abandonment by owners
  • Uncontrolled breeding
  • Lack of spay/neuter programs
  • Insufficient animal welfare infrastructure
  • Economic challenges preventing pet care
⚠️

Challenges They Face

  • Hunger and malnutrition
  • Exposure to harsh weather
  • Traffic accidents
  • Disease and infections
  • Human cruelty and neglect
  • Lack of medical care
💚

How You Can Help

  • Adopt a stray instead of buying
  • Provide food and water responsibly
  • Support local animal shelters
  • Report injured animals
  • Volunteer your time
  • Spread awareness
🍖

Feeding Responsibly

While feeding strays is compassionate, it's important to do it responsibly:

  • Feed at consistent times and locations
  • Clean up after feeding to maintain hygiene
  • Provide fresh water alongside food
  • Avoid feeding near busy roads
  • Use proper dog food when possible
  • Coordinate with neighbors to avoid overfeeding
🏡

Adoption vs. Buying

Why adoption is the better choice:

  • Save a life - shelters are overcrowded
  • Local dogs are healthier and more adaptable
  • Reduce demand for puppy mills
  • Adopted pets are often already vaccinated
  • Street dogs are incredibly loyal and grateful
  • Lower cost with higher emotional reward
✅

Do's Around Stray Dogs

  • Approach slowly and calmly
  • Let them sniff your hand first
  • Speak in soft, gentle tones
  • Give them space and respect their boundaries
  • Report sick or injured strays to authorities
  • Support vaccination and sterilization drives
❌

Don'ts Around Stray Dogs

  • Don't make sudden movements or loud noises
  • Don't corner or chase them
  • Don't disturb them while eating or sleeping
  • Don't attempt to pet aggressive dogs
  • Don't throw stones or harm them
  • Don't separate mother dogs from puppies
💉

Importance of Vaccination & Sterilization

Vaccination Benefits
  • Prevents rabies transmission to humans
  • Reduces spread of canine diseases
  • Creates healthier dog population
  • Protects community health
Sterilization Benefits
  • Humanely controls population
  • Reduces aggressive behavior
  • Prevents unwanted litters
  • Leads to healthier, longer lives

Pet Care Guide

Essential tips for keeping your furry friends happy and healthy

🍖

Feeding Guide

For Dogs:

  • Feed 2-3 times daily for adults
  • Puppies need 3-4 smaller meals
  • Provide fresh water at all times
  • Avoid chocolate, grapes, onions
  • Choose age-appropriate food

For Cats:

  • Feed 2-3 small meals daily
  • Cats prefer wet and dry food mix
  • Keep water bowls clean and full
  • Avoid dairy products
  • Don't overfeed - follow guidelines
✨

Grooming Tips

Regular Maintenance:

  • Brush coat regularly (daily for long hair)
  • Bathe dogs every 4-6 weeks
  • Trim nails every 2-3 weeks
  • Clean ears weekly
  • Brush teeth 2-3 times per week

Professional Care:

  • Professional grooming every 6-8 weeks
  • Annual dental cleaning
  • Regular skin and coat checkups
💉

Vaccination Schedule

Essential Dog Vaccines:

  • Rabies (required by law)
  • Distemper
  • Parvovirus
  • Hepatitis
  • Bordetella (kennel cough)

Essential Cat Vaccines:

  • Rabies
  • Feline Distemper (Panleukopenia)
  • Feline Calicivirus
  • Feline Herpesvirus

Start vaccinations at 6-8 weeks, with boosters every 3-4 weeks until 16 weeks old.

🧼

Hygiene Practices

Living Space:

  • Clean bedding weekly
  • Wash food and water bowls daily
  • Vacuum pet areas regularly
  • Disinfect litter boxes daily

Personal Hygiene:

  • Check for fleas and ticks regularly
  • Keep paws clean after walks
  • Maintain proper flea/tick prevention
  • Regular deworming schedule
🎓

Basic Training Tips

🐕 For Dogs

  • Start Early: Begin training at 8 weeks
  • Be Consistent: Use same commands always
  • Positive Reinforcement: Reward good behavior
  • Short Sessions: 5-10 minutes, multiple times daily
  • Basic Commands: Sit, Stay, Come, Down, Leave it
  • Socialization: Expose to different people and animals
  • Patience: Never punish - redirect instead

🐱 For Cats

  • Litter Training: Usually instinctive, keep box clean
  • Scratching Posts: Provide alternatives to furniture
  • Clicker Training: Cats respond well to clickers
  • Treat Motivation: Use high-value treats
  • Respect Independence: Train in short bursts
  • Play Sessions: 15-20 minutes daily minimum
  • Boundary Setting: Consistent rules for counters, etc.
